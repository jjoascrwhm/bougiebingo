The El Paso-Southern New Mexico Chapter

Excellence in leadership, service, and community impact

A Legacy of Leadership

Founded in 2018, the El Paso–Southern New Mexico Chapter of Jack and Jill of America, Inc. was established to bring the values and mission of Jack and Jill to the El Paso and Las Cruces communities. We are an organization of mothers dedicated to raising the next generation of African American leaders while enhancing the lives of all children.

Our mothers are committed to the development of children through cultural exposure, leadership training, and philanthropic service. Since our founding, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to nurturing the next generation while strengthening the bonds of our community.

Bougie Bingo represents our premier chapter-level fundraising initiative, directly supporting programs that transform lives. We invite you to join us in this elegant celebration of our shared commitment to excellence and impact.
